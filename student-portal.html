Teach the Teacher Training Outlook

3-Day Instructor Preparation

Use this as the quick instructor-facing path before delivering the 16-week course.

Day 1

Curriculum Overview

Walk through the five modules, the 16-week path, weekly discussions, CASEL survey checkpoints, assignments, and student submission flow.

Day 2

Practice the Methods

Model and rehearse the core routines: Pause, Label, Reframe, Respond; Pause, Options, Consequences, Choose; respectful communication; and repair.

Day 3

Facilitation and Review

Practice scoring, feedback, class discussion expectations, document review, pacing decisions, and how to coach students without doing the work for them.

Philosophy

Teach for Transfer

Students should not only understand the lesson. They should be able to use it in school, home, work, online, and community pressure moments.

Discussion Board

Weekly discussion posts and replies

Post your own answer first. After your post is submitted, classmates’ posts unlock. Then reply to at least two classmates before the week ends.

Discussion rules
  1. Read the week’s content and complete the week’s work first.
  2. Create your own post before viewing classmates’ posts.
  3. Reply to at least two classmates with specific encouragement, a question, or a useful connection.
Quiz Center

CASEL survey checkpoints

Complete the same CASEL self-efficacy survey in Week 1, Week 8, and the final week. Each submission goes to your instructor for scoring, growth comparison, and feedback.

How these quizzes work
  1. Week 1 shows your starting point before the course work begins.
  2. Week 8 shows midpoint growth and helps your instructor adjust support.
  3. The final week shows your end-of-course growth and receives final feedback.
