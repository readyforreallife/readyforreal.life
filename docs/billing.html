What this page is for

Use this page as the internal starting point for billing. From here, you can move to the secure Stripe tools you need without hunting through the homepage or broken shortcuts.

Billing Portal Best for subscriptions, invoices, payment methods, and customer billing management.
Stripe Dashboard Best for owner-level review of payments, products, links, revenue, and account-level settings.
Teacher Mode Records Use your registration and certificate folders inside Teacher Mode to see who signed up and what credentials have been issued.
Stripe’s secure tools cannot be fully hosted inside your website. The cleanest user-friendly option is what you have here: an on-site Billing Center that sends you to the secure Stripe page only when needed.
