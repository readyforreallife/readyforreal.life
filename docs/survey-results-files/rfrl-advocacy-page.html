Why This Program

Ready for Real Life Is a Direct Response to What the Audit Found

The audit identified student achievement gaps, a broken culture, and a district that rarely discusses what students actually need. The community survey showed that 98% of residents already know something is missing and 79% are ready to say so out loud to the board.

Ready for Real Life Instruction and Education is a research-backed curriculum built by two Tooele County residents — Mike and Mekenzi Terry — from nearly two decades of combined experience in law enforcement, youth work, education, and raising five kids in this community. It teaches conflict resolution, emotional regulation, accountability, and real-world decision-making as skills — not attitudes. It is built to complement what teachers already do, not add to their load.

The district already raised property taxes by 8% in 2025. Tooele County taxpayers deserve to see that investment move the needle. This program is one concrete step toward doing that.
