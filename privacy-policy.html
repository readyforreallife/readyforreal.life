Information We Collect

We collect information that you choose to provide when you contact us, register for programs, request access, submit a form, or make a purchase. Depending on the page or service, this can include:

  • Name, email address, phone number, organization, and role.
  • Registration details, notes, responses, or training interests you submit.
  • Access-code or licensing-related information needed to unlock protected materials.
  • Basic technical information provided by your browser or device, such as IP address, browser type, and page activity needed to operate the site securely.
  • Payment and billing information processed through Stripe or another payment provider acting on our behalf. We do not store full payment card numbers on this website.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to inquiries, provide requested information, and communicate with you.
  • Process registrations, deliver training, manage enrollment, and provide licensed access to protected materials.
  • Send confirmations, receipts, updates, and access-related communications.
  • Process payments, maintain billing records, and manage customer service requests related to purchases.
  • Improve the website, app, training materials, and support experience.
  • Protect the site, investigate misuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Parties to Whom Information May Be Disclosed

We may disclose information only as reasonably necessary to operate the business, fulfill a request, complete a transaction, or comply with the law. Parties may include:

  • Payment processors such as Stripe for checkout, billing, receipts, and transaction support.
  • Email, hosting, storage, and technical service providers used to deliver confirmations, site features, and secure access.
  • Internal founders, authorized staff, or contractors who need the information to provide the requested service or support.
  • Government, regulatory, or law-enforcement authorities when disclosure is required by law, court order, subpoena, or to protect rights, safety, or property.

Method of Disclosure

Information is disclosed by secure web submission, encrypted payment checkout, email communication, limited administrative access, and normal technical transmission between this website and the service providers that support site operations. We do not sell personal information.

Security Practices

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to help safeguard information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for website traffic.
  • Restricted administrative access to registration, billing, and protected-content systems.
  • Use of established third-party providers for secure payment and technical delivery services.
  • Reasonable efforts to limit stored information to what is needed for operations, communication, billing, and access control.

No website, app, or transmission method is completely risk-free, but we work to use practical safeguards appropriate to the services offered here.
