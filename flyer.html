About the Creator

For years I’ve worked in public service, supported youth in difficult moments, and raised five kids of my own. I returned to school for a Master of Arts in Teaching because I saw a gap: students need life skills that build respect, self‑control, and confidence in real situations.

This program exists to prepare young people and adults to handle pressure, repair mistakes, and choose the long view.
